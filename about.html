Our Story

Powering the world, one roof at a time.

We started Sunday because we believed that switching to solar shouldn't be a complex headache insted should be an upgrade for your home.

The Mission

Founded in 2024, Sunday was born out of a simple observation: the technology for clean energy was ready, but the experience of getting it wasn't. Most installations were clunky, the monitoring was confusing, and the design was an afterthought. We've combined Silicon Valley software with world-class renewable engineering to create a system that is as smart as it is efficient. Our goal is to empower 100,000 homes with energy independence by 2028.
